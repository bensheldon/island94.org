> I [Berggren] began looking into this as I found the whole premise of the GEP strange. Basically, it seems to be employed as follows: (a) Researchers think that some gender differences found in the West reflect innate and universal gender differences. (b) They find smaller such differences outside of the West. (c) This is taken as proof that the differences in the West are the truest revelation of such innate and universal gender differences.
